Why this helps

Best practices

  • Use clear item names and quantities.
  • Group items by section (produce, dairy, pantry).
  • Pin your weekly shopping list for quick access.

FAQ

Yes. Everyone can write in their own language and collaborators see translated text.

Do we need a separate translation service account?

No. Translation is integrated into the app configuration.
